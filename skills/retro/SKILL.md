# Retro — reflect and improve

## Steps

1. **What shipped** — scan the git log / merged PRs since the last retro.
2. **Health** — test-coverage trend, CI stability, recurring or flaky failures.
3. **Friction** — what slowed you down or recurred? Name the top one to three.
4. **Act** — turn each lesson into a concrete change: a `CONTEXT.md` gotcha, a new or edited
   skill, or a checklist item. Vague "do better" does not count.

## Gate

At least one concrete, written improvement lands — not just observations.
